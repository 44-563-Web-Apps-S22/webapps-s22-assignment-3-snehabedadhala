Berlin Beast

Game : Football
Animal : Sea Lion
Color : Black
Berlin beast is an official mascot for Berlin Football team. He performs various routines to entertain fans during football games. It is one of the highly recognised mascot in football games.

Fun Facts:

  • He appeared in many public events to entertain fans.
  • It is characterized by external ear flaps, long foreflippers, the ability to walk on all fours, short, thick hair, and a big chest and belly.
  • Among these characteristics, the one that most readily distinguishes it from regular seals is their ability to walk on all fours.
